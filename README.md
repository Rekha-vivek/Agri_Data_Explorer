### 1. Rice Production Trend (Top 3 States)

The leading rice-producing states show a consistent upward trend, with northern and eastern states contributing the majority of national production.

### 2. Wheat Yield Improvement

Top-performing districts demonstrate strong yield growth, indicating efficiency improvements rather than only land expansion.

### 3. Oilseed Growth

Oilseed production growth is uneven across states, highlighting the need for region-specific agricultural support.

### 4. Areaâ€“Production Relationship

Most districts show strong positive correlation, confirming that increased cultivated area generally leads to higher production.

### 5. Cotton Production Trend

Cotton production shows long-term growth with noticeable year-to-year fluctuations due to climatic and irrigation factors.

Groundnut cultivation is highly concentrated in a few productive districts.

Both crops show long-term growth, with rice displaying more stability and wheat showing higher variability.
